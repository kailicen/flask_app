About

The Buddyship Program aims to help you set short-term goals in Toastmasters and accomplish them within a certain time frame. More importantly, it is designed to make friends and increase accountability in achieving goals. Let's dive into your 'buddyship' journey!

You are expected to:
- Set your buddy
- Set your own goal (general & specific) and reward in achieving that goal
- Record your buddy's progress after each meeting

You can:
- View a history of your progresses (recorded by your buddy)
- Change your info including your goal and reward

Lastly, if you encounter any issues or have any suggestions, please feel free and contact us through

- Hobart Toastmasters Page
